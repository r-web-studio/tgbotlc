AVAILABLE COURSES
-----------------

1. IELTS Preparation Course
   Our comprehensive IELTS preparation course is designed to help you achieve your target band score. We cover all four modules: Listening, Reading, Writing, and Speaking. Our experienced instructors will teach you proven strategies and techniques to maximize your score.
   
   Duration: 3 months (48 sessions)
   Levels: Intermediate to Advanced (B1-C2)
   
2. General English Course
   Improve your everyday English skills! This course covers grammar, vocabulary, reading, writing, listening, and speaking. Perfect for those who want to communicate confidently in daily life, travel, or social situations.
   
   Duration: Ongoing (monthly enrollment)
   Levels: Beginner to Advanced (A1-C2)
   
3. Business English Course
   Master professional English for the workplace. Learn business vocabulary, email writing, presentation skills, meeting participation, negotiation techniques, and more. Ideal for professionals working in international companies.
   
   Duration: 2 months (32 sessions)
   Levels: Intermediate to Advanced (B1-C2)
   
4. Kids English Course
   Fun and engaging English lessons for children aged 5-12! Our interactive approach uses games, songs, stories, and activities to make learning English enjoyable and effective for young learners.
   
   Duration: Ongoing (quarterly enrollment)
   Levels: Beginner to Pre-Intermediate (A1-A2)
   Age Group: 5-12 years old

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS (FAQ)
---------------------------------

Q1: What English level do I need to start?
A: We offer courses for all levels, from complete beginner (A1) to advanced (C2). We conduct a free placement test to determine your current level and recommend the appropriate course.

Q2: How long does it take to prepare for IELTS?
A: It depends on your current level. For students at B1 level, we recommend 3-4 months. For B2 students, 2-3 months is usually sufficient. Our average student improves by 1.5 bands.

Q3: Do you offer trial lessons?
A: Yes! We offer a FREE trial lesson for all new students. You can experience our teaching style and meet the teacher before enrolling. Дарсдан аввал бепул синов дарси мавжуд. Sinfdan oldin bepul sinov darsi mavjud.

Q4: What is the class size?
A: Our standard classes have 8-12 students. Private lessons are one-on-one. We also offer semi-private lessons (2-4 students) at a discounted rate.

Q5: What payment methods do you accept?
A: We accept:
- Cash (UZS)
- Bank transfer (HUMO, UZCARD, Visa, Mastercard)
- Click payment system
- Payme payment system
- Online payment via our website
Накд пул, карталар ва Click/Payme орқали тўлов қилиш мумкин.

Q6: Can I get a refund if I'm not satisfied?
A: Yes, we have a satisfaction guarantee. If you attend the first 2 sessions and are not satisfied, we offer a full refund minus a 50,000 UZS administrative fee. After 2 sessions, no refunds are available, but you can transfer to another course or pause your enrollment.

Q7: Do you provide certificates?
A: Yes! Upon successful completion of any course, we provide an official Smart English Academy certificate. For IELTS preparation, we help you register for the official IELTS exam and provide a completion certificate.

Q8: Are there any discounts available?
A: Yes! We offer:
- Early bird discount: 10% off if you register before the month starts
- Group discount: 15% off for groups of 3+ students
- Family discount: 20% off for 2+ family members
- Student discount: 10% off with valid student ID
- Referral discount: 50,000 UZS off for each successful referral

Q9: What materials do you use?
A: We use internationally recognized textbooks (Oxford, Cambridge, Pearson) along with our own supplementary materials. All materials are included in the course fee. Дарсliklar va barcha materiallar to'lovga kiritilgan.

Q10: Can I switch courses after enrolling?
A: Yes, you can switch to another course within the first week of enrollment at no additional cost. After the first week, a transfer fee of 100,000 UZS applies, and any price difference must be paid.

Q11: Do you offer online classes?
A: Yes! We offer both in-person and online classes. Online classes are conducted via Zoom or Google Meet and follow the same curriculum as in-person classes. Online courses have a 10% discount.

Q12: What if I miss a class?
A: We understand that life happens. If you miss a class, you can:
- Join the next available batch for that session
- Watch recorded sessions (available for online students)
- Schedule a make-up session with the teacher
- Attend a catch-up class on Saturday (free for enrolled students)

Q13: How do I prepare for my first class?
A: Just bring yourself and a notebook! We provide all materials. For the placement test, no preparation is needed. We recommend arriving 15 minutes early on your first day.
